## 🚀 Projects

### AdventureWorks Business Intelligence
Brief Description:
This project analyzes the AdventureWorks dataset to evaluate overall business performance, including sales, products, and customer behavior. The goal is to transform raw data into meaningful insights that support strategic decision-making.

Tools Used:

Power BI
Excel

Key Insights:

Identification of top-performing products and categories
Analysis of sales trends over time
Regional performance insights
Customer segmentation and behavior patterns

### Maven Market Business Intelligence
Brief Description:
This project focuses on analyzing retail data from Maven Market to understand sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, and product trends. The dashboard provides a clear view of business performance to support data-driven decisions.

Tools Used:

Power BI
Excel

Key Insights:

Sales and revenue performance trends
Top-selling products and categories
Customer purchasing patterns
Regional and store-level performance analysis
Key business KPIs tracking
